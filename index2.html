The Mc-Laren Speedtail


The McLaren Speedtail is a limited-production hybrid sports car manufactured by McLaren Automotive. Succeeding the F1, the car is the fourth addition in the McLaren Ultimate Series, after the Senna, the P1, and the F1. The car is also part of the 18 new cars or derivatives that McLaren will launch as part of its Track22 business plan. The Speedtail was unveiled on October 26, 2018. Slated for release in February 2020, the car recharges while driving. Due in part to its use of cameras in lieu of side mirrors and no side-mounted airbags
